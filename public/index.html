About

Hi, welcome to Flippy! Flippy is an interactive website designed to stimulate deep, thought provoking conversation between two individuals. A person is able to choose a question from a topic area that interests them. They will then be paired with someone else who shares the same interest in debating that question. Each will be given a card where they can write down their initial thoughts on the question. After both have completed their response, they can 'Flippy' the card and see each others thoughts. At this point, the two individuals will enter a private chat room and can discuss their topics at length. To help support your argument we have provided a menu on the side filled with evidence from reputable sources to back up your statements. Once you are done with your discussion, you can simply end click 'End Flippy' and exit the chat.
